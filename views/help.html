Help

Quick answers for using Fertig smoothly.

Common Questions

  • How do I open my subject questions?
    Go to Question Bank and pick your year and subject.
  • How do tests work?
    Go to Tests, choose a subject, and start the timed paper.
  • How do I switch theme?
    Use the moon/sun button in the top-right corner.
  • How do I log out?
    Open the profile menu and click Logout.
