About Us

Fertig is a focused exam-prep workspace for students who want quick access to questions, tests, and study support.

What Fertig is for

Fertig brings question banks, timed tests, and revision tools into one place so students can spend less time searching and more time practicing.

How it helps

  • Question Bank:
    Browse subject-wise PYQs by year and topic.
  • Tests:
    Practice with timed subject tests and review your performance.
  • Study Material:
    Access structured revision resources in one flow.
  • Simple workflow:
    Move between practice, review, and support without leaving the platform.
